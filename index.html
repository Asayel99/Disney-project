Frozen 2

Elsa, Anna, Kristoff, Olaf, and Sven leave Arendelle to embark on a journey into the Enchanted Forest. They seek to discover the origin of Elsa's magical powers and save their kingdom, which is threatened by elemental spirits. The sisters uncover truths about their family's past and the conflict that angered the spirits, leading Elsa to accept her role as the Fifth Spirit and Anna to become the new Queen of Arendelle.

Encanto

tells the story of the magical Madrigal family, who live in a charmed house in Colombia. Every family member is blessed with a unique magical gift, except for Mirabel. When the family's magic begins to fade and their house starts to crack, Mirabel, feeling like an outsider, goes on a quest to find the source of the problem. She discovers that the magic is being weakened by the pressure her grandmother, Abuela Alma, puts on the family to be "perfect." By helping her family members embrace their true selves and confronting Abuela about her fears, Mirabel saves the magic.
